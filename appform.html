Adoption Application Form

Please answer the following questions carefully. Some of the questions are designed to help you reflect your decision to adopt a dog. We try to ensure the best possible match between a dog and adopter. Remember that this is a lifetime commitment.

Taking care of dogs require full responsibility and financial capacity. Hence, rescuers and posters prefer not to adopt dogs to minors. An adult must fill up this form and sign the adoption agreement.

1. Applicant Information
If rented, are you allowed to have dogs?
2. Pet History
Do you currently have pets?
If yes, how many?
Does any member of your household have any known allergies to animals?
If you have children, have they been taught how to behave with animals?
If you have other pets, have they all been vaccinated?
If you have other pets, are they all spayed/neutered?
Have you ever had a pet euthanized?
Have you ever lost a pet?
3. Adoption-specific Questions
Do all members of the family support your decision to adopt a dog?
How much time per day will you be able to interact with the dog?
Will your dog be alone at home?
4. Consent Agreement
 I acknowledge that by submitting this application, I agree to the screening process required to ensure I am qualified to adopt the dog.
I allow the rescuer/poster to conduct an occular inspection of my residence to ensure the safety of the dog to be adopted.
I am committed to taking full responsibility for the dog's health and welfare for the rest of its life, which could be 10 years or more.
I fully understand and agree to pay the Adoption Fees to the rescuer/poster upon custody transfer of the dog.

I consent to visiting rights of rescuer/poster should this application result to an adoption, to ensure the terms of the adoption agreement are being followed.
You have successfully submitted an application!

The rescuer/poster of the dog for adoption will get in touch with you to schedule a screening interview.
We wish you a fur-fect match, good luck!
